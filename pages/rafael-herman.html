Rafael Y. Herman:
A Dialect of Light in the Dark with ESSE


DRONE OPERATOR, CAMERA OPERATOR and VIDEO EDITOR



  Palazzo Sant'Elia • Palermo, Sicilia

Rafael Y. Herman’s “ESSE,” opened a dialogue with his audience on the nature of light and dark, showcased in an interactive display meant to be viewed at night. Presented at Palermo’s historic Palazzo Sant’Elia, the show featured 16 monumental photographs depicting surreal landscapes, which Herman obtained through unique circumstances—their unnatural colors and alternate realities were captured in the dark.
“ESSE” was on view at Fondazione Sant’Elia from April 22—June 22, 2022.
Piazzetta della Messinese, 6, 90133 Palermo PA
